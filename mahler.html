Mahler's Symphony No.2

ASK:
Create a campaign for the Jacobs School of Music’s biggest performance of the year: Gustav Mahler’s Symphony No. 2.

INSIGHT:
College students generally aren’t chasing classical music, but they will show up for a spectacle. The focus of the campaign isn't genre, instead promote the sheer magnitude of a 300-piece orchestra.

CONCEPT:
Play with the limitations of the medium to prove the scale of the event. If the orchestra is too big to fit on a poster, a screen, or an ad, it’s definitely something you have to see in person.

(Note: My campaign won the pitch and was rolled out across campus newspapers, digital signage, and social media.)
