Fall for Arkansas

ASK:
Position Arkansas as a premier fall destination for millennials and families, shifting the perception that it's only a summer spot.

INSIGHT:
Millennials and families are twice as likely to travel if there's an event to show up for.

CONCEPT:
We turned the entire state into a two-month festival. Fall for Arkansas featured weekly experiential activations partnered with major outdoor brands, giving visitors a reason to plan a trip.
