Little 500

ASK:
Lead the creative direction for the Little 500 also known as "The World’s Greatest College Weekend". The event required a visual identity that matched the massive scale and legacy of the competition.

INSIGHT:
For years, the race’s branding relied on the university’s traditional cream and crimson. To make the event stand on its own, we needed to embrace the colors of the race.

CONCEPT:
I built a distinct brand system derived entirely from the primary colors of the race flags.
