Cinzano 100

ASK:
Organize a 100-mile race modeled after the famous scene from the 1979 film Breaking Away, establishing a connection between the movie's "Team Cinzano" and our collegiate team.

INSIGHT:
Riders are more likely to commit to a 100-mile trek if it feels like they are participating in a piece of local film history rather than just a standard long ride.

CONCEPT:
We created a 100-mile route around Bloomington that visited actual filming sites from the movie. To make it a proper event, we included feed stations, media coverage, and a post-ride cookout to celebrate the winners.
