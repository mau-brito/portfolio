About me

To stay balanced, I spend a lot of time on a bike, most notably racing in the Little 500, the largest cycling race in the country. Outside of school, I’m usually reading and traveling; I’ve been to 26 countries, but Mexico, where I was born and raised will always be home.

Exhibit Columbus: Yes And

ASK:
Promote Exhibit Columbus, a program that commissions designers to build site-responsive installations around the city’s iconic architecture.

INSIGHT:
The exhibition’s theme was "Yes, And"—the improv rule of accepting the current reality and building upon it.

CONCEPT:
The poster established the "Yes" by showing the landmarks in their original state, while my photos captured the "And", documenting the transformation of those spaces.
