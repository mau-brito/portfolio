Native American Heritage Month

ASK:
Design a campaign to promote a month-long series of events for Native American Heritage Month.

INSIGHT:
Cultural advertising is delicate. I needed a visual language that respected the culture without reducing it to a set of symbols.

CONCEPT:
I designed a pattern system that evokes the traditional art but applies a modern twist that kept the campaign feeling current and celebratory rather than purely historical.
