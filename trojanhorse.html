Trojan Horse

Ask

Bring awareness to The Trojan Horse, a Bloomington restaurant with over 75 years of service and one of the last three restaurants in town standing since the 50s.

Insight

Longevity doesn't always equal credibility. However, our research uncovered that 12 out of 70 people we interviewed had a first date there, and 10 ended up married to that person.

Concept

The "Right" Date. Leverage the restaurant's intimate, speakeasy atmosphere and its statistically improbable success rate to position The Trojan Horse as the place you go when you want the date to go right.
