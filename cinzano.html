About me

I’m driven by the kind of ideas that feel effortless but are obsessively crafted. My background spans photography, illustration, and graphic design, but I do my strongest work when working in a team. To stay balanced, I spend a lot of time on a bike, most notably racing in the Little 500, the largest cycling race in the country. Outside of school, I’m usually reading and traveling; I’ve been to 26 countries, but Mexico, where I was born and raised will always be home.

Cinzano

ASK:
Sell merchandise for Team Cinzano, a collegiate cycling team that ironically positions themselves as full-time athletes who just happen to be part-time students.

INSIGHT:
Standard calls-to-action get scrolled past.

CONCEPT:
The campaign used visual repetition, writing "I will Cinzano Merch" over and over to playfully badger fans into making a purchase.
